Cayenne S

The Porsche Cayenne S is one of the most appealing luxury SUVs. It boasts impeccable construction, intense features, and a surprisingly useful cabin. While it lacks a third row, its sliding, reclining, and folding rear seat offers a degree of versatility that other luxury SUVs lack.

With the new 2013 Cayenne Diesel model and the carry-over S Hybrid, good fuel economy also becomes a strong selling point, while maintaining the decades of high-performance expectations associated with a Porsche.

Performance Metrics

Despite purists' lamentations, the Cayenne is Porsche to its core, being one of the most rewarding and involving SUVs to drive on the highway. Handling is exceptional, improving further with certain high-tech chassis options. The Turbo model is even quicker than many sports cars.

Technical Specifications

Engine & Performance (2013 S)

  • Engine Type: 4.8-liter V8 (Naturally Aspirated)
  • Max Power: 400 hp @ 6,500 rpm
  • Max Torque: 369 lb-ft @ 3,500 rpm
  • 0-60 mph: 5.4 seconds
  • Top Speed: 165 mph (266 km/h)

Dimensions & Economy

  • Length: 4,846 mm
  • Width: 1,939 mm
  • Height: 1,705 mm
  • Curb Weight: 2,065 kg
  • Fuel Economy (EPA Rated): 16/22 mpg City/Highway
