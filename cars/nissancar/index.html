Patrol

The Nissan Patrol is a series of full-size SUVs related by Nissan in Japan and sold throughout the world since 1951. It is available as either a short-wheelbase (SWB) two-door or a long-wheelbase (LWB) five-door chassis. The Patrol is sold in Africa, Central and South America, the Middle East, parts of Southeast Asia, and Western Europe, as well as New Zealand and Australia. For the 2011 model year, it was introduced in North America as the upscale Armada (renamed Infiniti QX80).

Performance Metrics

The Patrol is powered by a 5.6-L V8 engine. Nissan has created a heft willing and able to take you anywhere, whether you are cruising the city or turning down the road less travelled. The V8 engine, while sounding rough for a family SUV, still excels at towing and tough off-roading. Raw power and Nissan reliability lie at the very heart of this legendary SUV.
