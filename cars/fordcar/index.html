Lincoln MKC

The 2015 Lincoln MKC sits in the middle of the luxury compact SUV class. It has a very appealing cabin, two strong engine options, and crisp and lively handling, but some of its rivals bring more to the table. The Lincoln MKC is produced by Lincoln. Lincoln launched the MKC concept at the 2013 LA Auto Show and the production model officially went on sale in June 2014. The MKC is built upon the Ford Global C platform, shared with the Ford Escape small SUV. The 2015 model is assembled at Louisville Assembly Plant.

Performance Metrics

The 2015 Lincoln MKC comes standard with a 240-horsepower turbocharged (EcoBoost) 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine. A 285-horsepower EcoBoost 2.3 liter four-cylinder engine is available. Both engines are mated to a six-speed automatic. The EPA estimates that the MKC gets $20/29 \text{ mpg}$ city/highway, which is average for the class. Models with the upgraded engine come standard with all-wheel drive, and accelerate strongly off-the-line and deliver solid highway passing power.

  • Max Torque: $305 \text{ lb-ft} @ 3,000 \text{ rpm}$
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)
  • Transmission: 6-speed Automatic

Dimensions & Economy
